Must Visit Places

JAIPUR
Jaipur, the "Pink City" of Rajasthan, is famous for its stunning architecture, including Hawa Mahal and Amber Fort. Founded in 1727, it offers rich culture, vibrant bazaars, and traditional cuisine, making it a must-visit destination for history and art enthusiasts.

OOTY
Enjoy the serene hills and beautiful gardens of Ooty.Ooty offers stunning attractions like Ooty Lake, Doddabetta Peak, and the Botanical Gardens, perfect for nature lovers. Don’t miss the charming Nilgiri Mountain Railway and the scenic Emerald Lake for a tranquil experience.

GULMARG

Gulmarg, a picturesque hill station in Jammu and Kashmir, is famous for its lush meadows, stunning views, and excellent skiing opportunities in winter. The Gulmarg Gondola offers breathtaking views and access to the surrounding snow-capped peaks.

LADAKH

Ladakh, known for its stunning landscapes and unique culture, features breathtaking sites like Pangong Lake and Nubra Valley. Explore ancient monasteries, enjoy adventure sports, and experience the vibrant local traditions in this Himalayan paradise.

VANARASI

Varanasi, one of the oldest cities in the world, is famous for its spiritual significance and ghats along the Ganges River. Experience the vibrant culture, stunning rituals, and ancient temples that make it a unique pilgrimage destination.

CHANDRATAL

Chandratal, or Moon Lake, is a stunning high-altitude lake in Himachal Pradesh, known for its mesmerizing blue waters and breathtaking mountain scenery. Ideal for trekking and camping, it offers a serene escape into nature's beauty.

TAWANG

Tawang, nestled in Arunachal Pradesh, is renowned for its breathtaking monasteries like Tawang Monastery and stunning landscapes, including Sela Pass and Bumla Pass. Enjoy the serene atmosphere and rich Tibetan culture amidst majestic mountains.
